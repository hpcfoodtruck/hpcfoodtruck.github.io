HPC International School presents

THE FOOD TRUCK

Welcome to our new and improved food truck! We listened to what students wanted, made prices cheaper, and simplified everything.

See how it works

"Instead of selling things nobody wants, we're focusing on exactly what you actually feel like buying."

We keep our menu simple with essentials like fresh snacks, chips, and cold drinks—all at prices cheaper than any shop nearby.

The Three Big Changes

Simple, easy, and made for you

01

Focusing on What You Actually Want

We ask you what snacks you want on the menu every single week. Instead of a fixed menu that never changes, we adapt to what you feel like having.

02

Easier Ways to Pay

Right now, we accept Apple Pay. Very soon, we'll also have card readers to accept Revolut, Visa, and Mastercard so you don't always need to carry cash.

03

A Simpler Menu

Our menu is now split in two: basic favorites (like chips, cold drinks, and standard snacks) that are always available, plus one special treat (like fresh cookies) that changes every single week based on what you love.

When & Where
Your Ideas

Tell us what to stock

We'll keep a feedback notebook at the truck. Just write down any snacks or drinks you want us to stock, and we'll do our best to get them!

Location

Where we park

You can find us right by the school bike racks, under the shade of the trees next to the benches.

When we are open

Opening Hours

We are open during lunch break, from 1:00 PM to 1:40 PM every day.

What we want to achieve

Our simple goals for the school community

01

Cheap Prices

We want to provide affordable snacks, treats, and cold drinks directly to everyone in school.

02

Easy Payments

We want to make buying things simple and quick with modern mobile and card payment options.

03

No Fancy Nonsense

No weird or overcomplicated items. Just good, simple snacks that students actually enjoy eating.

04

Save Time and Money

A much cheaper and faster choice than walking all the way to the expensive supermarkets outside.

Who's running this?

Get in touch
with us.

Have a question, an idea, or want to help out with the club? Just come chat with us near the bike racks during lunch break, or reach out to our supervising coordinator.
